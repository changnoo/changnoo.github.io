Robotics Control System Simulation: Open Loop & Closed Loop Controllers.

I implemented a Two-Link Manipulator Robot Dynamics on MATLAB, then implemented Closed-Loop controllers: PD + Gravity Controller in the Joint Space, Stiffness Controller in the Task Space. I explored the benefits and drawbacks of each controllers, and simulated both systems.

Technologies Used: MATLAB, Simulink

Amazoom: Automated Warehouse Simulation Multithreaded & Real-Time simulation in C#

Amazoom, an Amazon clone, simulates a warehouse with products, robots, and trucks working together to serve clients in real-time. Orders are placed ofcontainer our web interface; robots and trucks work synchronously to restock and deliver products; and status is constantly updated through WPF GUI.

Click on the gif to the left to learn more about the project & be taken to the GitHub repository.

Technologies Used: C#, ASP.NET, MVC webpage, MongoDB and WPF.
